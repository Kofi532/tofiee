Project funding.


The following the processes invloved to request funding for shovel ready projects

  • 1. The real estate developer with a shovel ready projects send us letter of intent stating their funding request.
  • 2. All documents concerning the project must be sent to tofiee designated email for review.
  • 3. After the review land the project is considered viable for funding, online meeting with be scheduled to discuss the project with our affiliated funding raising company.
  • 4. The affiliate company will send an engagement to the developer and a contract will be signed between the two parties.
